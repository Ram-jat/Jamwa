Welcome to Jamwa.in - Your Special Place for Real Silver Jewelry

Our Story

We started in Jaipur, making and selling beautiful silver jewelry. At first, we sold to shops, but now we sell directly to you!

  • Jaipur Roots: Our story begins in Jaipur, a city famous for its silver. We make special 92.5 silver jewelry here. Jaipur’s legacy of crafting silver is in everything we create.
  • From Big to You: We used to sell to big shops, but now we want you to have our jewelry. That's why we’re selling directly to customers like you.
  • Made and Delivered by Us: We make our jewelry and deliver it to you using our own courier service. That way, it gets to you quickly and safely.
  • Making It Easy for You: We changed things up so you can buy our amazing silver jewelry online. It's easy and fast. Just pick what you love, and we'll send it straight to you.
